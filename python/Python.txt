5. While Loop
Question: Jab tak stock > 0 hai, stock ko 1 se kam karo aur print karo. End mein "Out of Stock" print karo.

stock = 3
while stock > 0:
    stock = stock - 1
    print(f"{stock} Item Sold")

print("Out of Stock")
